Area 53 at Baltimore Regionals

The Cow Abductors made an impressive showing at the Baltimore Regional robotics competition. Area 53 competed impressively, well outperforming the expectations of the team. Overall, Area 53 took somethingth place.

  1. Welcome!

    June 12th, 2012
    By Nick Y.

    Area 53 is the Robotics team located at Eleanor Roosevelt High School in Greenbelt, MD. The Cow Abductors consists of approximately 50 students who work together to develop, design, and ultimately create innovative solutions to a variety of problems. We strive to encourage creativity and teach real-world skills to our students.

  2. Competition

    June 14th, 2012
    By Nick Y.

    We are proud annual participants of the FIRST FRC competition and the ION Mini-Urban Challenge. Additionally, our team also assists others in competitions such as FIRST's FLL competition.
