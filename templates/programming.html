  1. Programming Team (System Controls)

    The Programming Subteam of the ERHS Engineering Club is responsible for a wide variety of functions of the team. Under the Programming/System Controls Subteam there are several miniteams. These include Software Engineering, and Electrical Engineering. Additionally, the programming team works with the business team to help develop the club website.

  2. Software Engineering

    The largest and most important part of System Controls is software engineering. The programmers are in charge of making the robot able to move. Some goals of the software engineering department are to develop stable drive code, develop code to control end effectors, and develop artificial intelligence systems.

  3. Electrical Engineering

    The electrical engineering department's primary responsibility is wiring physical components. We put a major emphasis on making sure the wiring is neat, organized, and most importantly, SAFE.
